# Reference Board: Public site v1

## Source pattern matrix

| Page beat | TravelConnect | Global Ehsan | As-Sābiqūn |
|---|---|---|---|
| Trust | Contact/credibility utility bar | Date, account and primary donate action | Phone, amanah message and service CTA |
| Hero | Focused promise, two actions | Campaign-led headline and immediate action | One Islamic-services promise, Explore + How it works |
| Choice | Package tiers and featured cards | Quick Donate and category cards | Four clear service cards |
| Reassurance | Three-step booking process | Impact/accountability story | Three-step Amanah trail |
| Evidence | Reviews | Reports, field media and service facts | What customers receive; no fabricated proof |
| Close | Consultant CTA and contact block | Strong donate CTA and deep footer | Begin a service, phone/contact, service links |
